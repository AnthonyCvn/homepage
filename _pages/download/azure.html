ReductStore Server

ReductStore is available as an IoT module on the Azure Marketplace. This module allows you to deploy ReductStore directly to your IoT Edge devices via the Azure portal.

How to Deploy:

  • Go to ReductStore on Azure Marketplace
  • Click on "Get It Now"
  • Sign in to your Azure account.
  • Choose a plan - either Enterprise or Community.
  • Select your subscription, IoT Hub, and edge device.
  • Click "Create" and wait for the deployment to complete.
